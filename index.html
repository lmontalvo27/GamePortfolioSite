About Me

I'm Luis Montalvo, a Computer Science graduate and indie game developer passionate about crafting immersive, story-driven horror and fantasy worlds. I developed and shipped Menticide, a survival horror game, on Steam using Unreal Engine. Currently, I’m creating a dark fantasy game blending psychological depth and epic lore, while building full-stack web applications.
